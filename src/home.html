What To Do?

  1. Pick an origin station by clicking the drop-down list beside "Origin"
  2. Pick a destination station by clicking the drop-down list beside "Destination"
  3. Pick a type of ride by clicking the drop-down list beside "Type of Ride"
  4. Click "Submit" button once done
  5. Click "Reset" button if you want to start again
